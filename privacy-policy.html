Privacy Policy

Who We Are

Hamilton Lawn Mowing is owned and operated by Kowhai Galvin, located at 4/94 Norton Road, Frankton, Hamilton, New Zealand. We provide lawn mowing and property maintenance services across Hamilton and surrounding areas.

What Information We Collect

We collect personal information when you contact us, request a quote, or make a booking. This includes your name, phone number, email address, and property address.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide quotes, confirm bookings, and communicate with you about our services. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third Party Tools

We use Calendly for online bookings and Google Workspace for business operations. These services have their own privacy policies which we encourage you to review.

Your Rights Under the Privacy Act 2020

Under the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020, you have the right to access the personal information we hold about you and request corrections if it is inaccurate. To make a request, contact us at galvinkowhai@gmail.com or call 0210 838 7863.

Data Storage

Your information is stored securely and only retained for as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, contact Kowhai Galvin at galvinkowhai@gmail.com or 0210 838 7863.
